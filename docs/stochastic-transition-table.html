5 Stochastic Transition Table

Each row below describes a transition: the left-hand side indicates the compartment being updated, the specific term is the rate-based term, and the stochastic transition is given as a binomial draw from the source population with probability \(1-\exp(-r)\).

Difference Equation Term Specific Term Stochastic Transition (Binomial Draw)
